Gerês National Park: hike and swim

Natural pools in Gerês National Park is a formation of small pools in the river surrounded by the amazing landscape of the National Park. In the summer with the low level of water in the river it makes amazing pools where you can have a great swim and sectione in these extremely transparent waters. A true must do, especially in warm summers.

Douro Valley: with winery visit and wine tasting

Douro Valley is a region has been designated by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site. Traditionally, the wine was taken downriver in flat-bottom boats called “rabelos”, to be stored in barrels in cellars in Vila Nova de Gaia, Porto. In this experience we will show you the Port wine route, since the place where the grapes grow and are smashed (Douro Valley) to the place where is stowed to get old and later on consumed or exported to the world (Porto city) in a very relax and informal way which will make you feel a local.

Spoken languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish and French (Please confirm spoken languages for each scheduled tour)
